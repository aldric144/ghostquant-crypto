# AML Training Program

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Classification:** Confidential - Internal Use Only

---


This document establishes the comprehensive AML training program for GhostQuant™ personnel. Effective AML compliance requires knowledgeable, well-trained staff who understand their responsibilities and can identify suspicious activities.

**Regulatory Basis:** 31 CFR 1020.210(b)(2)(iii) - Training requirement

---



**Frequency:** Annual (minimum)  
**Duration:** 2 hours  
**Format:** Online or in-person  
**Completion Required:** Within 30 days of hire, then annually

**Topics:**
- AML/CFT fundamentals
- Money laundering typologies
- Red flags and indicators
- Reporting suspicious activity
- SAR confidentiality
- Individual responsibilities
- Consequences of non-compliance
