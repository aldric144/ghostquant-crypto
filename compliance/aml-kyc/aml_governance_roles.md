# AML Governance Roles and Responsibilities

**Document Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2025  
**Classification:** Confidential - Internal Use Only

---


This document defines roles and responsibilities for AML/KYC compliance at GhostQuant™. Clear role definition ensures accountability, effective oversight, and comprehensive compliance coverage.

**Regulatory Basis:** 31 CFR 1020.210(b)(2) - Designation of AML compliance officer

**Position:** Chief Compliance Officer / Chief AML Officer  
**Reports To:** Chief Executive Officer  
**Direct Reports:** Deputy AML Officer, Investigation Lead, Compliance Manager

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Overall AML program management and oversight
- Regulatory liaison and reporting
- SAR filing authority and approval
- Policy and procedure development
- Risk assessment oversight
- Board and senior management reporting
- Regulatory examination coordination
- Budget and resource management

**Specific Duties:**
- Approve all AML policies and procedures
- Review and approve all SAR filings
- Conduct annual AML risk assessment
- Provide quarterly reports to Board
- Coordinate with regulators and law enforcement
- Oversee independent testing
- Approve high-risk customer relationships
- Manage AML training program
- Investigate complex or high-profile cases
- Represent company in regulatory examinations

**Qualifications:**
- 10+ years AML/compliance experience
- CAMS certification required
- Law degree or advanced compliance certification preferred
- Cryptocurrency/FinTech experience required
- Management experience required

**Authority:**
- SAR filing decisions
- Customer relationship termination
- Policy approval
- Budget allocation
- Hiring and termination of compliance staff
- Direct access to Board and CEO

---


**Position:** Deputy Chief AML Officer  
**Reports To:** Chief AML Officer  
**Direct Reports:** Senior AML Analysts

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Assist Chief AML Officer in program management
- Day-to-day operational oversight
- Alert review and investigation coordination
- Quality assurance
- Training coordination
- Technology oversight

**Specific Duties:**
- Review and triage high-priority alerts
- Oversee investigation queue management
- Conduct quality assurance reviews
- Coordinate training delivery
- Manage compliance technology
- Assist with regulatory examinations
- Act as Chief AML Officer in their absence
- Supervise AML analysts
- Review SAR drafts before AML Officer approval
- Coordinate with business units

**Qualifications:**
- 7+ years AML/compliance experience
- CAMS certification required
- Cryptocurrency experience preferred
- Supervisory experience required

**Authority:**
- Alert assignment and prioritization
- Investigation oversight
- Quality assurance decisions
- Training approval
- Technology change approval (with AML Officer)

**Position:** Senior AML Investigator  
**Reports To:** Chief AML Officer  
**Direct Reports:** AML Investigators, Forensics Specialists

**Primary Responsibilities:**
- Complex investigation management
- Investigation team supervision
- SAR narrative development
- Law enforcement coordination
- Forensic analysis oversight

**Specific Duties:**
- Lead complex and high-profile investigations
- Supervise investigation team
- Review and approve SAR narratives
- Coordinate with law enforcement
- Conduct forensic analysis
- Develop investigation procedures
- Train investigators
- Testify in legal proceedings (if required)
- Manage case management system
- Conduct look-back reviews

**Qualifications:**
- 8+ years investigation experience
- CFE or CAMS certification required
- Law enforcement or forensic accounting background preferred
- Cryptocurrency investigation experience required

**Authority:**
- Investigation assignment
- SAR narrative approval (subject to AML Officer final approval)
- Law enforcement coordination
- Subpoena response (with legal review)
- Expert witness testimony

- **A (Accountable):** Ultimate accountability, approves work
- **C (Consulted):** Provides input, subject matter expert
- **I (Informed):** Kept informed of progress/decisions

**Critical Positions:**
- Chief AML Officer
- Deputy AML Officer
- Investigation Lead

**Succession Plan:**
- Deputy AML Officer → Acting Chief AML Officer
- Senior Analyst → Acting Deputy AML Officer
- Investigation Lead → Cross-trained with Deputy

**Documentation:**
- Succession plan documented and approved by Board
- Annual review and update
- Cross-training program in place
- Knowledge transfer procedures

---


**Minimum Staffing:**
- At least one qualified AML Officer available at all times
- At least two analysts available for alert investigation
- 24/7 on-call rotation for critical alerts

**Remote Work:**
- All compliance staff equipped for remote work
- Secure VPN access
- Remote access to all systems
- Virtual collaboration tools

---



**Daily:**
- Critical alerts to AML Officer
- Sanctions matches to AML Officer

**Weekly:**
- Alert queue status to Deputy AML Officer
- Investigation status to Investigation Lead

**Monthly:**
- Compliance metrics to AML Officer
- SAR filing summary to AML Officer

**Quarterly:**
- Comprehensive compliance report to Board
- Risk assessment updates to senior management

**Annually:**
- AML program assessment to Board
- Independent testing results to Board

**Regulatory:**
- SARs to FinCEN (as required)
- OFAC blocking reports (within 10 days)
- Regulatory examination responses (as requested)

- SAR follow-up (as requested)
- Subpoena responses (with legal review)
