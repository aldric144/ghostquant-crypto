This policy establishes procedures for handling data subject rights requests under GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

---



**Description:** Individuals have the right to know what personal data is collected and how it is used

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- Privacy notice published on website
- Notice provided at data collection
- Transparent processing documentation
- Annual privacy disclosures

**Timeline:** At or before collection

---


**Description:** Individuals can request access to their personal data

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- DSAR access request form
- Identity verification required
- Comprehensive data export
- Secure delivery method

**Timeline:** 
- GDPR: 30 days (extendable to 60 days)
- CCPA: 45 days

---


**Description:** Individuals can request correction of inaccurate data

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- DSAR correction request form
- Identity verification required
- Assessment of correction request
- Implementation of corrections
- Notification to third parties (if applicable)

**Timeline:** 30 days

---


**Description:** Individuals can request deletion of their personal data

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- DSAR deletion request form
- Identity verification required
- Assessment of legal obligations
- Secure deletion procedures
- Certificate of destruction

**Exceptions:**
- Legal obligation to retain (AML/KYC: 5 years)
- Legal claims
- Public interest
- Archiving purposes

**Timeline:** 30 days (if no exception applies)

---


**Description:** Individuals can request limitation of processing

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- DSAR restriction request form
- Identity verification required
- Assessment of restriction grounds
- Implementation of processing restrictions
- Notification before lifting restriction

**Grounds:**
- Accuracy contested
- Processing unlawful
- Data no longer needed but required for legal claims
- Objection pending

**Timeline:** 30 days

---


**Description:** Individuals can request their data in a structured, machine-readable format

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- Data export in JSON or CSV format
- Structured data format
- Secure delivery
- Verification procedures

**Scope:** Data provided by the individual, processed by automated means, based on consent or contract

**Timeline:** 30 days

---


**Description:** Individuals can object to processing based on legitimate interests

**GhostQuant™ Implementation:**
- DSAR objection request form
- Identity verification required
- Assessment of objection
- Cessation of processing (unless compelling legitimate grounds)
- Documentation of decision

**Grounds:**
- Legitimate interest processing
- Direct marketing
- Scientific/historical research

**Timeline:** 30 days

---



**Primary Data:**
- Blockchain addresses (pseudonymous, public information)
- Transaction patterns (public blockchain data)
- Market data (public information)
- Risk indicators (derived from public data)

**Limited Personal Data:**
- KYC information (when required for compliance)
- Biometric data (for identity verification)
- Investigation data (for AML compliance)

---


**Principle:** Honor data subject rights even when not strictly required

**Rationale:**
- Builds trust
- Exceeds regulatory requirements
- Prepares for regulatory evolution
- Demonstrates privacy commitment

**Implementation:**
- Data subject rights procedures implemented
- Request forms available
- Clear timelines
- Transparent communication

---



**Channels:**
- Email: privacy@ghostquant.com
- Web form: ghostquant.com/privacy-request
- Mail: GhostQuant Privacy Office, [Address]

**Acknowledgment:** Within 2 business days

---


**Purpose:** Prevent unauthorized disclosure

**Methods:**
- Government-issued ID
- Account credentials
- Security questions
- Biometric verification (for high-sensitivity requests)

**Standard:** Reasonable verification appropriate to risk

---


**Categories:**
- Access
- Correction
- Deletion
- Restriction
- Portability
- Objection
- Opt-out (CCPA)

**Priority:**
- Standard: 30 days
- Complex: 60 days (with notification)
- Urgent: 10 days (security incidents)

---


**Questions:**
1. Is the requester the data subject?
2. What data is requested?
3. Does an exception apply?
4. What is the legal basis for processing?
5. Can we comply?

**Exceptions:**
- Legal obligation to retain
- Legal claims
- Public interest
- Archiving purposes
- Freedom of expression

---


**Access Requests:**
- Compile all personal data
- Provide in accessible format
- Include processing information
- Secure delivery

**Correction Requests:**
- Verify accuracy issue
- Implement corrections
- Notify third parties
- Document changes

**Deletion Requests:**
- Verify no exception applies
- Secure deletion
- Verify deletion
- Certificate of destruction

**Other Requests:**
- Implement as appropriate
- Document actions
- Notify requester

---


**Contents:**
- Acknowledgment of request
- Action taken
- Timeline
- Exceptions (if applicable)
- Appeal process
- Contact information

**Delivery:**
- Secure email
- Encrypted portal
- Registered mail (if requested)

---


**General Rule:** No fee for first request

**Exceptions:**
- Manifestly unfounded requests: Reasonable fee
- Excessive requests: Reasonable fee
- Additional copies: Reasonable fee

**Fee Schedule:**
- Access request (additional copy): $25
- Portability request (additional copy): $25
- Complex requests: Actual cost (documented)

---


**Grounds:**
- Manifestly unfounded
- Excessive
- Legal exception applies
- Unable to identify requester

**Process:**
1. Document reason for refusal
2. Legal review
3. Data Protection Officer approval
4. Notify requester within 30 days
5. Explain reason
6. Inform of appeal rights

---


**Process:**
1. Requester submits appeal
2. Independent review
3. Data Protection Officer decision
4. Notify requester
5. Inform of regulatory complaint rights

**Timeline:** 30 days

---

- Request details
- Identity verification
- Assessment
- Actions taken
- Response
- Appeals

**Retention:** 3 years

**Storage:** Secure database, Genesis Archive™

**Frequency:** Annual

- Exceptions
- Timelines
- Escalation

- Refusal rate
