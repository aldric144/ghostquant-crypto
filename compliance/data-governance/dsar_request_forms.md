1. **Receipt:** Acknowledge within 2 business days
2. **Identity Verification:** Verify requester identity within 5 business days
3. **Assessment:** Assess request and determine action within 10 business days
4. **Response:** Respond to requester within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA)
5. **Documentation:** Log all actions in Genesis Archive™
6. **Retention:** Retain DSAR records for 3 years


- **Access:** 30 days (GDPR), 45 days (CCPA)
- **Correction:** 30 days
- **Deletion:** 30 days (if no exception)
- **Objection:** 30 days
- **Restriction:** 30 days


- **Complex requests:** Escalate to Data Protection Officer
- **Legal questions:** Escalate to Legal Counsel
- **Denials:** Require Data Protection Officer approval
